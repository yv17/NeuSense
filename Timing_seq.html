Timing and Sequence Illusion

Illusion description

What is it? In this illusion, there is an overall passage of musical notes. The passage can be separated into subsections that contain notes of a similar pitch structure. You will hear that pauses are inserted into the overall passage and it is your job to try and remember the passage.



It is very likely that when the pauses are inserted so that the subsections are clearly separated it is easy to remember. This is opposed to when the pauses are inserted within the subsections (so that they are not clearly separated) and this makes it more difficult to remember the passage.
